Detailed Description

For processing and storage of information on CirclePack variables.

For processing and storage of information on CirclePack variables of the "slider" type. This goes in the "Pack Info -> Variables" tab in the lower panel. They are specified, when setting variables, by starting with "[SLIDER {optional}]" and ending with their value, which must be a double. See 'SliderControlPanel' and 'SliderPanel'.
