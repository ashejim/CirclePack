Detailed Description

A JTextField for entering/displaying formated real.

A JTextField for entering/displaying formated real values. Can specify 3 to 15 digit length (default 8) and specify a title that goes above the field. If no title is given, this just serves to format a number. This field allows formulas in $..$ format.
