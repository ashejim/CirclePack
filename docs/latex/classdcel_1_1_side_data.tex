\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Side-\/pairing data for boundary edges in multiply-\/connected DCEL structures. \textquotesingle{}Side\+Description\textquotesingle{}. 

This is for DCEL structures; compare to \textquotesingle{}Side\+Description\textquotesingle{}. A "{}side"{} of a complex is a segment of faces in \textquotesingle{}red\+Chain\textquotesingle{}. A "{}side-\/pairing"{} occurs only when a complex is not simply connected and then it refers to a pair of connected edge segments, each maximal with the property that their lists of red edges are identified in the complex. In the non-\/simply connected case there may also be "{}border"{} segments (maximal connected but unpaired segments).

A linked list \textquotesingle{}\doxylink{classdcel_1_1_pack_d_c_e_l_a1c832f1296fdd952256519fe21a35893}{Pack\+DCEL.\+pair\+Link}\textquotesingle{} of \textquotesingle{}\doxylink{classdcel_1_1_side_data}{Side\+Data}\textquotesingle{}s encodes the side-\/pairing information.

Caution:\+ \textquotesingle{}mob\textquotesingle{} element can easily be out of date. Likewise, the \textquotesingle{}center\textquotesingle{} information on the circles along the red chain are kept in the \textquotesingle{}\doxylink{classcombinatorics_1_1komplex_1_1_red_edge}{Red\+Edge}\textquotesingle{}s, and these are updated during \textquotesingle{}layout\textquotesingle{} operations. To update \textquotesingle{}mob\textquotesingle{} one generally has to start by updating the \textquotesingle{}\doxylink{classcombinatorics_1_1komplex_1_1_red_edge}{Red\+Edge}\textquotesingle{}s. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
