\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{classcanvasses_1_1_my_canvas_mode}{My\+Canvas\+Mode} is used to associate modes/\+cursors/\+icons to. 

\doxylink{classcanvasses_1_1_my_canvas_mode}{My\+Canvas\+Mode} is used to associate modes/\+cursors/\+icons to \textquotesingle{}\doxylink{classcanvasses_1_1_active_wrapper}{Active\+Wrapper}\textquotesingle{}s. It holds the icon, \textquotesingle{}mode\+Type\textquotesingle{} of creation, (e.\+g., from \textquotesingle{}My\+Tool\textquotesingle{}), and catches mouse events. Canvas modes are kept in vectors in \textquotesingle{}\doxylink{classcanvasses_1_1_active_wrapper}{Active\+Wrapper}\textquotesingle{} and persist. Mouse events are caught in \textquotesingle{}\doxylink{classcanvasses_1_1_active_wrapper}{Active\+Wrapper}\textquotesingle{}, but processed in \textquotesingle{}Active\+Mode\textquotesingle{}, including decisions about repaints.
