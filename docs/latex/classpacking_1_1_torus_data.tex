\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Routines for computing and storing data on a packing. 

Routines for computing and storing data on a packing that is a 1-\/torus. We assume that the radii have been computed. On creation of a \doxylink{classpacking_1_1_torus_data}{Torus\+Data} object, the torus will be adjusted to standard normalization with two side-\/pairings which have been updated.

For flat tori, first corner is at origin, next is at 1, and side-\/pairings are translations z -\/\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} z+a, z -\/\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} z+b.

For affine tori, common fixed point of the side-\/pairing maps is at the origin and the first corner is at 1. Thus the side-\/pairing maps are z -\/\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} az and z -\/\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} bz.

There are also static routines for working with tori.
