\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
For processing and storage of information on \doxylink{classall_mains_1_1_circle_pack}{Circle\+Pack} variables. 

For processing and storage of information on \doxylink{classall_mains_1_1_circle_pack}{Circle\+Pack} variables of the "{}slider"{} type. This goes in the "{}\+Pack Info -\/$>$ Variables"{} tab in the lower panel. They are specified, when setting variables, by starting with "{}\+[\+SLIDER \{optional\}]\+"{} and ending with their value, which must be a double.
