\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{Bary\+Point}{p.}{classbary_stuff_1_1_bary_point} is a triple of barycentric coords describing a. 

A \doxyref{Bary\+Point}{p.}{classbary_stuff_1_1_bary_point} is a triple of barycentric coords describing a point relative to a triangle; typically, a point in a triangle, on an edge, or at a corner. At creation we enforce b0+b1+b2=1. However, we do not require that the bj are all non-\/negative; negatives imply the point is outside the triangle.
