\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
DCEL face:\+ a triangular face bounded by three half-\/edges. 

Each face stores a pointer to one of its bounding half-\/edges ({\ttfamily \doxyref{edge}{p.}{classcombinatorics_1_1komplex_1_1_dcel_face_acce42f1d33f978183380fbfe2770284c}}); the other two are reachable via {\ttfamily edge.\+next} and {\ttfamily edge.\+prev}. The face lies to the {\itshape left} of its half-\/edge.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The {\ttfamily \doxyref{edge}{p.}{classcombinatorics_1_1komplex_1_1_dcel_face_acce42f1d33f978183380fbfe2770284c}} pointer and {\ttfamily \doxyref{face\+Indx}{p.}{classcombinatorics_1_1komplex_1_1_dcel_face_abb13a4191f755ec807f9a6f5f01c6565}} are not persistent — they may be reassigned when the alpha vertex is reset or the DCEL is restructured. Typically, {\ttfamily edge} is chosen so that its origin and the next vertex are already placed, and the third vertex is computed from them.
\end{DoxyNote}
Ideal (exterior) faces have {\ttfamily face\+Indx $<$ 0}.
