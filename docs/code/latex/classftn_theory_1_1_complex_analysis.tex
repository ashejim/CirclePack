\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Discrete complex analysis computations on circle packings. 

Compute complex analytic-\/like data for discrete analytic function f:\+ domain\+Data -\/-\/$>$ range\+Data. Computations put discrete complex derivative or other results into \textquotesingle{}output\+Data\textquotesingle{}. For instance, \textquotesingle{}diff\textquotesingle{} stores centers of the complex derivative of f.

On startup, parent packing is converted to euclidean and copied into \textquotesingle{}domain\+Data\textquotesingle{}.
