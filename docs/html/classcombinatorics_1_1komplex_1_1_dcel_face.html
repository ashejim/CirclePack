Detailed Description

DCEL face: a triangular face bounded by three half-edges.

Each face stores a pointer to one of its bounding half-edges (edge); the other two are reachable via edge.next and edge.prev. The face lies to the left of its half-edge.

Note
The edge pointer and faceIndx are not persistent — they may be reassigned when the alpha vertex is reset or the DCEL is restructured. Typically, edge is chosen so that its origin and the next vertex are already placed, and the third vertex is computed from them.

Ideal (exterior) faces have faceIndx < 0.
